Presentations and Proposals

A brief summary of the my talks and active observation time proposals.

AstroSat Proposal

A Cycle 14 (AO14) proposal has been submitted for observation time with Prof. Ravi Joshi as the PI.I am one the Co-I's in the proposal. The AstroSat data would be used to test cosmological simulations with spatially resolved properties of nearby starburts, AGNs and post starburst galaxies.

LAE & LBG's : Implications on z > 6 Reionization

I presented on detection and state-of-art understanding of high-z galaxies using various techniques and the challenged. The presentation was given as part of end of course presentation. I attended the course "Cosmic Dawn" by Prof. Hamsa Padmanabhan at University of Geneva via online mode.

Unveiling the dust from formation of galaxies

The talk was part of MS Talk series of Aakashganga IISER Pune. The talk was aimed at giving a brief introduction to my master's research. It was an interactive session organised with fresh undergraduate students at IISER Pune.

Effect of dust on luminosity function at high-z galaxies

The talk was part of weekly research report meetings at SNS Pisa. As a part of the Cosmology group, I presented recent developments in my research.
